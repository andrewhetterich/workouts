Hill running protocol — read before you go
Foot watch: foot/calf score dropped from 7 to 6 last week. Hills + parents' running surface = higher impact than lakefront. Steps to protect:
1. Extra calf warm-up: 20 calf raises (instead of 15) + ankle CARs before running
2. Don't bomb the downhills. Downhills hammer the shins and feet way harder than uphills. Shorten stride and let the hill do the work. If you feel ANY shin tension, walk.
3. Frozen water bottle on the plantar after the run
4. Distance target: 4-5 mi, not 7. Hills add stress.
5. HR will spike on uphills — that's fine. Walk the steep ones if needed.

Trainer note · Critical clarifications
You asked: is 3 sec down OR up?

Goblet Squat: 3 sec DOWN, 1 sec PAUSE at bottom, normal speed UP.
RDL: 3 sec DOWN (the lowering is the hamstring work). Normal speed UP.
Leg Extension: Normal speed UP, 3 sec DOWN.
Leg Curl: Normal speed CURL, 3 sec slow LOWER. This is the hamstring eccentric — most important for injury prevention.

Rule of thumb: the eccentric (lowering) is ALWAYS the 3-second portion. Concentric (lifting) is always normal speed.

Bird dog dropped — you noted it was too easy + felt it in lower back. Copenhagen plank stays.

BenchmarkDeload⏱ 55 min
1-mile time trial at the start (fresh legs after deloaded week). Then a 2-round light circuit (was 4 rounds at heavy weight). Ab finisher cut to single set each.
🎯 BENCHMARK TEST — 1-Mile Time Trial
Lakefront flat section, or FFC treadmill if >75°F. Run 1 mile as fast as you sustainably can. Strategy: don't go out too hot — your tempo pace is ~9:00/mi at RPE 6, so target somewhere around 7:30-8:30/mi for the TT. Quarter-mile splits help: try negative splits (each quarter faster than the last).

Block 1 mile baseline. This sets the bar for race pace work in Block 2 if you choose to train for distance.
